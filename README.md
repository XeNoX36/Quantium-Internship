**Data Analysis and Insights:**  
1. There was increase in sales in the lead-up to Christmas and there were zero sales on Christmas day itself. This is due to shops being closed on Christmas day.  

2. Most sales are coming mainly from Budget - older families, Mainstream - young singles/couples, and Mainstream - retirees.  

3. There are more Budget - Older families, Mainstream - young singles/couples   and Mainstream - retirees who buy chips.  

4. Older families and young families in general buy more chips per customer.  

5. Mainstream midage and young singles/couples are more willing to pay more per packet of chips compared to their budget and premium counterparts. This is also supported by there being fewer premium midage and young singles and couples buying chips compared to their mainstream counterparts.  

6. The THINS and DORITOS brands was the most sort after brand of chips, upto 53% of customers purchased these Brands  

7. The most Preferred Pack size for Mainstream-YOUNG SINGLES/COUPLES is 175g followed by 170g  


**Actionable Recommendations**  

**1. Optimize Inventory and Supply Chain**  
- Ensure sufficient stock levels of popular chip brands and pack sizes before Christmas to meet increased demand.  
- Manage inventory levels effectively to minimize waste while maximize sales.  

**2. Target Key Demographics**  
- Develop targeted marketing campaigns to reach Budget - older families, Mainstream - young singles/couples, and Mainstream - retirees, highlighting the value and convenience of chip products.  
- Use language and imagery that resonates with these demographics to increase brand engagement and loyalty.  

**3. Pricing Strategy**  
- Consider introducing premium products or pack sizes with higher price points to capitalize on the willingness of Mainstream midage and young singles/couples to pay more.  

**4. Product Offerings**  
- Ensure availability of popular pack sizes, such as 175g and 170g, to meet customer demand and consider developing new products or flavors that cater to the preferences of key demographics, such as healthier options for older families.  

**5. Brand Promotion**  
- Emphasize the popularity of THINS and DORITOS brands in marketing campaigns to drive sales and increase brand loyalty.  
- You could also consider running in-store promotions, such as discounts or special offers, to drive the sales of these brands.
